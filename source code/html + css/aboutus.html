Our Story

Discover the journey behind Campus Tuck and our commitment to serving the student community

Welcome to Campus Tuck

Founded in 2015, Campus Tuck began as a small kiosk in the university commons, with a simple mission: to provide students with convenient access to essential snacks, drinks, and supplies at affordable prices.

What started as a humble operation has grown into the most popular campus store, serving thousands of students each week across three locations on campus.

Our success comes from understanding student needs - we're run by students, for students. Our team knows exactly what you need to power through lectures, late-night study sessions, and everything in between.

Our Mission & Values

Student-First Approach

We prioritize student needs above all else, offering products at prices that won't break your budget and convenient locations across campus.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through eco-friendly packaging and responsible sourcing practices.

Community Support

A portion of every sale goes back into student programs and campus initiatives that make our university a better place.
