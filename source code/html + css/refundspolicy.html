Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

Our Refund Policy

At Campus Tuck, we strive to provide high-quality products and excellent service to our campus community. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Please review our refund policy below to understand your options.

Important: Refunds are only available for eligible items with proof of purchase (receipt) within the specified time frame.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund or exchange if:

  • You received a damaged or defective product
  • You received the wrong item
  • The product doesn't match its description (for non-perishable items only)

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Present your original receipt or order confirmation
  • Return the item within 24 hours of purchase
  • Return the item in its original packaging (when applicable)

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Perishable goods (food items, beverages, etc.)
  • Opened or partially consumed products
  • Gift cards or prepaid meal plans
  • Items purchased on final sale or clearance
  • Personal hygiene products (when opened)

Refund Process

Here's how our refund process works:

1

Contact Us

Visit our store or contact us within 24 hours of your purchase to initiate a refund request.

2

Return Item

Bring the item and your receipt to our store for inspection by our staff.

3

Approval

Our team will verify the issue and approve eligible refunds.

4

Refund Issued

Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

  • Cash purchases: Refunded in cash
  • Campus meal cards: Credit applied to your card
  • Credit/Debit cards: Refunded to original card
  • Mobile payments: Refunded to original account

Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund after 7 business days:

  • Check with your bank or payment provider first
  • Contact your credit card company as processing times vary
  • If you've done these steps and still haven't received your refund, contact us at refunds@campustuck.edu

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange eligible items for:

  • The same product in a different size or color (when available)
  • A product of equal value
  • Store credit when an exact replacement isn't available

Exchanges are subject to product availability and must be made within 24 hours of purchase with receipt.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Do not use the product
  • Contact us immediately (within 24 hours of purchase)
  • Bring the item and packaging to our store for inspection
  • We'll replace the item or issue a full refund

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened food items unless they are defective.

Student Discount Refunds

Refunds for items purchased with student discounts:

  • Will be calculated based on the discounted price paid
  • Require valid student ID verification at time of refund
  • Cannot be applied to future purchases as credit

Contact Us

For questions about our refund policy or to initiate a refund:

  • Email: refunds@campustuck.edu
  • Phone: 0315-1622934 (ask for Refund Department)
  • In Person: Visit our store during business hours

Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday, 9AM-5PM to assist with refund requests.
