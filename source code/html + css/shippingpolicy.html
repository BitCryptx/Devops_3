Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

1. Overview

At Campus Tuck, we strive to provide efficient and reliable shipping services to our campus community. This policy outlines our shipping methods, delivery times, fees, and other important information regarding the delivery of your orders.

Note: Our shipping services are currently available only within the campus premises and select nearby student housing areas.

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options tailored to campus needs:

Service Delivery Time Description Fee
Standard Campus Delivery 1-2 business days Delivery to dorm rooms or campus buildings Rs. 50
Express Delivery Same day (if ordered before 3PM) Priority handling for urgent needs Rs. 100
Pickup Station Ready in 4 hours Collect from our campus store Free
Classroom Delivery Scheduled between classes Delivery to your classroom between lectures Rs. 75

3. Order Processing

Here's what happens after you place an order:

Order Received

We confirm your order via email/SMS within 15 minutes of placement.

Processing

Our team prepares your items within 2 hours for standard orders, or immediately for express orders.

Quality Check

Every order undergoes a final quality check before dispatch.

Dispatch

Your order is assigned to a delivery agent with real-time tracking available.

Delivery

Our agent delivers to your specified location and confirms completion.

4. Delivery Areas & Restrictions

We currently serve:

  • All campus dormitories and residential halls
  • Academic buildings during operating hours
  • Designated pickup points across campus
  • Approved off-campus student housing within 2km radius

Restrictions:

  • No deliveries during final exam weeks to academic buildings
  • Limited delivery hours in residential halls (8AM-10PM)
  • Certain fragile items may have restricted delivery locations

5. Shipping Fees & Free Shipping

Our shipping fees are structured to cover our campus delivery operations:

  • Free Shipping: Available for orders over Rs. 1000 or for members of our loyalty program
  • Special Discounts: 50% off shipping for bulk orders from student organizations
  • Subscription: Monthly unlimited shipping pass available for Rs. 300

All fees are clearly displayed during checkout before payment.

6. Tracking Your Order

We provide multiple ways to track your order:

  • Real-time Map Tracking: Follow your delivery agent's location in our app
  • SMS Updates: Automatic notifications at key stages
  • Order Portal: Check status anytime on our website
  • Customer Service: Call or chat with our team for updates

7. Delivery Issues & Failed Deliveries

If we encounter issues delivering your order:

  • Three delivery attempts will be made for each order
  • After failed attempts, items will be held at our campus store for 3 days
  • Unclaimed orders will be returned and refunded (minus shipping fees)
  • Incorrect addresses may incur a redelivery fee

Pro Tip: Ensure your campus location details are accurate and include building names, room numbers, or specific landmarks to help our delivery team.

8. Holiday & Special Event Shipping

During these periods, please note:

  • Reduced delivery hours during campus holidays
  • Extended processing times during orientation week and finals
  • Special delivery arrangements available for campus events (request 48 hours in advance)
  • No deliveries on official campus closure days

9. International Shipping

Currently, we only offer shipping within the campus vicinity and nearby approved student housing. For international shipping needs, please contact our special services team at shipping@campustuck.edu.
