Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Campus Tuck ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our campus store services, including our website and in-store operations.

Note: By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Full name and student ID number
  • Contact information (email, phone number)
  • Campus affiliation and department
  • Payment information (processed securely, not stored)

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage data and purchase history
  • Anonymous demographic information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Details
Service Provision Process transactions and fulfill orders
Communication Send order confirmations and service updates
Improvement Enhance our products and services
Security Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
Compliance Meet legal and campus requirements

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Campus Administration: For verification of student status and campus-related services
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in payment processing and delivery (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger or sale of company assets

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for online transactions
  • Secure storage protocols
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Until account deletion request
  • Transaction records: 5 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Inactive accounts: 2 years after last activity

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@campustuck.edu.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
